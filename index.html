How to use this to-do List

To add a task, type it in, default priority is low. Since nobody is productive working from home.
To check a task, simply click on the task and it will turn to "Checked status"
To erase a task, simply click on the cross on the right hand side.

Quick FYI, I did throw the task priority in here, but it's implemented as a colour shceme: Green, Yellow, Red, Magenta Red.
